I am an assistant professor at Department of Computer Science of Stevens Institute of Technology. .

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Computer Science Department at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Chengxiang (“Cheng”) Zhai and co-advised by Prof. Tao Xie. Prior to UIUC, I received my B.S. degree from the Institution of Interdesciplinary Information Sciences (Yao class) in Tsinghua University in China, 2012.

Research Interests

  • I am interested in text mining, information retrieval, natural language processing and their application to software engineering and security. Here are a list of topics I am working on:

      • Natural language processing for software engineering: Assisting software developers for natural language generation for software testing, information retrieval for NL/code data [NL4SE@FSE 2018], pre-processing and tokenization for NL-code data.

      • Automated machine learning for natural language processing: Assisting data scientists for automated hyperparameter optimization [ACL-IJCNLP 2021], also checkout [FLAML/nlp], our open-source tool for automated hyperparameter optimization for NLP tasks. We currently support 4 tasks: sequence classification, sequence regression, multiple choice and summarization.

      • Natural language processing for security: Assisting software developers to explain Android permission purposes [VL/HCC 2018, RE 2018], vulnerability reports [MLHat@KDD 2021], etc.
